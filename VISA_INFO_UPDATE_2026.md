### 5. **E23 Working KITAS** (Work Permit)
**Official Name:** E23 Work Visa with IMTA (Work Permit)

**Key Updates:**
- ‚úÖ Duration depends on position:
  - Directors/Commissioners/Senior Managers: **12 months** (extendable)
  - Advisors/Consultants: **6 months** (non-extendable)
- ‚úÖ Company capital requirement: Minimum **IDR 1 billion per foreign worker**
- ‚úÖ Foreign worker levy (DPKK): **USD 100/month** (paid by worker)
- ‚úÖ Employer levy (DKP-TKA): **USD 100/month** (paid by company, pre-paid)
- ‚úÖ Minimum salary: Typically **IDR 25-30 million/month** (varies by industry)
- ‚úÖ Experience requirement: Minimum 5 years relevant experience
- ‚úÖ Processing time: 2-3 months total
  - RPTKA (Foreign Worker Plan) ‚Üí IMTA (Work Permit) ‚Üí E-Visa (5-10 days)
  - Onshore KITAS processing: 4 weeks after arrival
- ‚úÖ Government fees: IDR 3-8 million (excluding agent fees)
- ‚úÖ Total cost estimate: USD 1,000-1,500

**Sources:**
- [Comprehensive Guide to Work Permit Indonesia 2026 - Flado](https://flado.id/comprehensive-guide-to-obtaining-a-work-permit-in-indonesia-rptka-notification-and-kitas/)
